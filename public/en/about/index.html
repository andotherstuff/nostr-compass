About

Nostr Compass is a technical resource for the Nostr protocol, providing developers, relay operators, and builders with the information they need to stay current on protocol evolution.

We publish weekly newsletters covering NIP proposals, client updates, relay developments, and notable code changes. Our podcast features conversations with the developers whose work is covered in each newsletter. The topic index documents key Nostr concepts with links to primary sources and our coverage.

If you’re building on Nostr and would like to contribute or appear on the podcast, contact us at info@nostrcompass.org.

Funding

Nostr Compass does not exist to make a profit, and all materials and documentation produced are released under the MIT license.

We appreciate donations and support in any form. If you’d like to help sustain this project, please get in touch.

Contributors

All material produced by Nostr Compass is open source and released under the MIT license. Anyone is welcome to contribute by opening issues and pull requests, reviewing newsletters and other material, and contributing translations.
